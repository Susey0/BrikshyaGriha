Tree & Forest Activities For Kids

Engaging kids with nature is an important way to foster environmental consciousness...

Activities to Try:

  • Tree Identification: Teach kids to identify different tree species by their leaves and bark.
  • Forest Scavenger Hunt: Create a list of natural items for kids to find.
  • Plant a Tree: Involve kids in planting trees and taking care of them.

Encouraging kids to spend time outdoors helps them appreciate nature and understand the importance of trees.
